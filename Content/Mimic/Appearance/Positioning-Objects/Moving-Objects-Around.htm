Moving Objects

After an object is added, you can move it around the frame to place it where you need it. You can do this by dragging the object.

Hove to Move an Object

  1. Open a frame to which you have added objects.
  2. Hover over the object until the cursor becomes an arrow .

  3. Click on the object and drag it to the appropriate location on the frame.

You can also float or sink objects if you have multiple objects in a frame that overlap one another. Floating and sinking objects brings them forward or backward in the layer order. See Floating and Sinking Objects.

You can also align objects. See Aligning Objects.
