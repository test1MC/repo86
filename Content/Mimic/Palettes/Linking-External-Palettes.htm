Linking to External Palettes

How to Link to an External Palette

  1. Select View > Palettes.

    The Palettes window pane opens.

  2. In the local toolbar of the Palettes window pane, click .
  3. In the Open dialog, find and select the palette you want to open.
  4. Click Open. The linked palette opens in the Palettes window pane.
