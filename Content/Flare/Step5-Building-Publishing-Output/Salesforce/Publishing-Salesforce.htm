Publishing to Salesforce®

After everything is set up and you have created a destination file, you can associate that file with a Clean XHTML target. Then you can build and publish the output.

How to Publish to Salesforce®

Following are steps for copying output files to Salesforce. For more information, and other parts of the process, see Salesforce® Publishing.

  1. Open your Clean XHTML target.
  2. Select the Publishing tab.
  3. Next to your Salesforce® destination, select the Publish check box.
  5. In the local toolbar, click Build.
  6. After the target is finished generating, click Publish in the local toolbar.
