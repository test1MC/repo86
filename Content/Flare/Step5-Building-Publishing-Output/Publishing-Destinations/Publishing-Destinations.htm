Publishing Destinations

In most cases, there is a little bit of setup work to complete initially when it comes to publishing your output. But once you are finished with the setup, the ongoing act of publishing your output files can be done with the click of a button.

An exception to this is when you are publishing to MadCap Central. If your project is bound to Central and you are logged in, there is no need to create a destination; it simply displays in the target. All you need to do is select it. See Associating Publishing Destinations with Targets.

A destination XML file has an .fldes extension and is stored in the Project Organizer under the Destinations folder.
