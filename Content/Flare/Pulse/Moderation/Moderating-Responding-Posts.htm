Moderating Responding Posts

If you are a moderator in Pulse, you can respond to other users. These are some of the ways you can respond in Pulse:

  • Responding to Articles You can respond to an article posted by another user. See Responding to Articles.
  • Responding to Questions You can respond to questions posted by other users in Pulse. See Responding to Questions.
  • Responding to Status Updates You can respond to status updates posted by other users. See Responding to Status Updates.
  • Responding to Tasks You can respond to task assignments posted in topic feeds. See Responding to Tasks.
