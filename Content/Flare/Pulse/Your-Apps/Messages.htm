How to Read and Reply to a Message

  1. Navigate to your user feed.
  2. In the Your Apps menu, click Messages. Your messages appear in the list.
  3. In the Messages page, click one of the messages in the list to open the conversation thread.

  4. (Optional) If you want to reply to a message, choose from these options:

    • Type a reply in the space provided.

      AND/OR

    • Click the paperclip to add an attachment.

  5. Click Reply.

How to Hide a Conversation

  1. Navigate to your user feed.
  2. In the Your Apps menu, click Messages. Your messages appear in the list.

  3. In the Messages page, click the Hide Conversation link (the x) to remove it from the page.
