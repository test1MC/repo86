Continuing the Sequence of Lists

When you apply the Continue Sequence option to a list, not only will that list continue the numbering of the previous list, but it will also grab the style attributes from the other list.

How to Continue the Sequence of Lists

  1. Place your cursor somewhere in the lower list (where you want to continue the numbering).

    • Ribbon Select Home > (down arrow).
    • Right-Click Right-click the ol or ul tag bar of the lower list (where you want to continue the numbering).
  3. Select Continue Sequence.
