You can add alternate text and screen tips for an equation so that it becomes accessible for disabled individuals in the output. You can also create a report that shows accessibility suggestions, which includes equations in your project that are missing alternate text. See About Accessibility and Reports.

When an equation is used in a heading and that heading is referenced in any way (e.g., cross-reference, table of contents entry), the equation is stripped from the reference.
