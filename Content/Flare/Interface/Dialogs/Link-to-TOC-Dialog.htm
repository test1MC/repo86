Link to TOC Dialog

This dialog is used to link entries in a table of contents (TOC) to another TOC or link entries in a browse sequence to a TOC. For more information see About Tables of Contents.

How to Open This Dialog

  1. Open a browse sequence or TOC.
  2. Select an entry and click .
  3. In the Properties dialog, click the Select TOC button.

Buttons and Sections

Toggles between showing or hiding the subfolders holding the TOC files.

Splits the dialog into two halves. The right half shows TOC files. The left side shows the subfolder holding the files.

Shows or hides hidden files and folders.

Moves up one folder.
