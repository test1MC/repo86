      3. If you want the background image to repeat, click to the right of background-repeat and select one of the options (definitions from http://www.w3.org).
        • inherit The image will inherit the style used by the parent tag.
        • no-repeat The image is not repeated: only one copy of the image is drawn.
        • repeat The image is repeated both horizontally and vertically.
        • repeat-x The image is repeated horizontally only.
        • repeat-y The image is repeated vertically only.
