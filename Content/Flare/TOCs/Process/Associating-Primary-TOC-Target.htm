Associating a Primary TOC With a Target

 

The following steps show you how to associate a primary TOC with a target.

How to Associate a Primary TOC With a Target

  1. From the Project Organizer, open the target.
  2. On the General tab in the Target Editor, click the drop-down arrow in the Primary TOC field, and select the TOC that you want to associate with the target.
