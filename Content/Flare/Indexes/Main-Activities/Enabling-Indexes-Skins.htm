Enabling Indexes in Skins

After inserting index keywords, you need to enable the Index feature in the skin that you intend to use for your target.

How to Enable an Index in a Skin

  1. In the Project Organizer, open an HTML5 Tripane skin or a Standard skin.
  2. On the General tab, place a check mark in the Index box.

After you have finished enabling indexes in a skin, you need to make sure the skin is associated with a target. See Associating Skins with Targets.
