Micro Content and Snippets

Micro content phrase/response combinations are not the same as snippets. In addition, micro content and snippets should not be thought of as an either/or proposition. It is good to use both in your project. However, micro content does have relationships with snippets in a few ways.

Featured Snippets

See Featured Snippets—Using Micro Content in Search Results.

Linking to Regular Snippets

When creating micro content, one of the methods you can use is to link a phrase to a regular snippet in the project.

See Response Method 2: Linking Phrases to Entire Topics or Snippets.

Inserting Regular Snippets

Another way to create a micro content response is to enter content directly in the Micro Content Editor. This can include inserting regular snippets in the editor, just as you would in a normal topic.

See Response Method 4: Entering Responses Directly in the Micro Content Editor.
