Building and Publishing the Flare Output

building and publishing the flare output

When building the output, you may see a message that states, "Some community features will not be displayed in tablet and mobile outputs." This means that when users are viewing your responsive output on a tablet or phone, they will not see comments or the Community tab. However, Pulse still tracks hits on pages in those formats for reporting purposes. You can hide the warning if you do not want to see it in future builds.
