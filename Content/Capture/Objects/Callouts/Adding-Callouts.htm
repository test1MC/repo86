Adding Bubble Callouts

The quickest way to create a callout is to use a bubble graphic.

How to Create a Bubble Callout

  1. Capture or open an image.
  2. Select Object > Objects > Graphics > Bubble.
  3. Click in the image and drag the mouse to draw a rectangle. Release the mouse button when you are finished.
  4. With the object selected, you can begin typing to add text to it. Click outside the object when you are finished.
  5. Click and drag the small orange circles around the edge of the callout to resize it as necessary.
  6. Click somewhere in the middle of the bubble and drag it to the appropriate location on the image. If you click the small bubble icon within the shape, the entire shape moves, except the pointer. If you click elsewhere inside the shape, the entire shape moves, including the pointer.
  7. Click the small orange circle at the tip of the pointer on the bubble shape and drag it to the appropriate location.
  8. (Optional) You can double-click the callout and use the tabs in the dialog to make various changes to it (see Bubble Properties Dialog). Click OK when you are finished.
