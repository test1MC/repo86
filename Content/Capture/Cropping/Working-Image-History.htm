Working With Image History

You can choose whether or not to save the history of cropped images.

How to Enable or Disable the Discard History Feature

  1. Open an image.

  2. In the Edit ribbon, either enable or disable Discard History.

    • Enabled When Discard History is enabled, the areas you crop out of an image are deleted. Once you save and close an image, you will not be able to retrieve the image history.

    • Disabled When Discard History is disabled, you will be able to recover the image history and resize the cropped image at any time, even after saving and closing an image. However, previously discarded regions will no longer be available.
