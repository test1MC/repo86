Enabling the Magnifier

You can enable or disable the magnifier that appears when you capture new images (using the Region method). The magnifier shows a close-up view of the area drawn by your cursor. This lets you more accurately capture a specific area. However, if you are using a computer that is relatively slow, you may wish to disable the magnifier.

How to Enable the Magnifier

  1. Select File > Options. The Options dialog opens.
  2. Select the Capture tab.
  3. Select Enable Magnifier.
