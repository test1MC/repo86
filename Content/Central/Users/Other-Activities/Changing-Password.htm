Changing Your Own Password

How to Change Your Password

  1. At the top of Central, click your avatar or name, and select User Settings.
  2. On the left, select Email & Password.
  3. On the right, click the Password tab.
  4. Complete the Current Password field.
  5. Complete the New Password field.
  6. Complete the Confirm Password field.
  7. Click Save.

If you are an administrator and need to change the password for another user, see Resetting Another User's Password.
