Opening the Reports Page

You can access the Reports page after opening a project in Central, or by using a widget.

Methods for Opening the Reports Page

There are multiple ways to open the Reports page to view it:

  • Reports Option on Projects Page After opening a project in Central, you can click Reports at the top.

  • Project Grid Context Menu On the Projects page, you can click the three dots on the left side of the row and select Reports.

  • Report Widgets If you have added Report widgets to a dashboard, you can click any of the square objects related to a specific type of report.
