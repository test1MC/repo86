Filtering Widgets

How to Filter a Widget

  3. In the menu, click the drop-down arrow next to the area you want to filter (e.g., Project, User, Priority), and make your selection(s) under it. If there are multiple drop-downs, Central will automatically expand the next one when you make your selection in the first drop-down. Radio buttons (circles) mean that you can make one selection, while check boxes mean that you can make multiple selections.
  4. When you are finished, click off the menu.
