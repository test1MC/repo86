Integrating Jira With Central

Integrating Jira with Central means completing the following sequential activities.

  1. Creating a Jira Cloud Account
  2. Creating a Jira App
  3. Setting Up Jira on the Central License
  4. Associating a Central User Account With a Jira User Account
  5. Associating a Central Task Board With a Jira Project
