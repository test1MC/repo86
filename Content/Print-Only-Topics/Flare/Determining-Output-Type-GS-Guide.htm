Developing Targets

The first task in developing a target for your project is to determine which type of output is most appropriate for your needs. You might even need to produce multiple outputs and require more than one output type. After you decide on the kinds of outputs, you can add targets based on those formats and editing the settings for each.

For more detailed information about developing targets—including examples—see the online Help, the Targets Guide, or the Print-Based Output Guide.
