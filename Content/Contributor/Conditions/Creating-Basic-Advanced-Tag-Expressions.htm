Creating Basic and Advanced Tag Expressions

You might create tag expressions when you are editing a document (by clicking on the left side of the local toolbar in the XML Editor). Doing this would simply let you see how a document would look when certain conditions are included or excluded, or when they are associated with a particular Flare target that the author has created.
