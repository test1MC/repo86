Autonumbers

You might use autonumbers in new documents that you create, or you might see autonumbers in templates or topics sent to you by Flare authors.

Why Would I Want to Use Autonumbers?

Following are just a few reasons for using autonumbering. For samples of formats that you might use for these purposes, see Autonumber Format Examples.

  • Paragraphs You can apply autonumbering to different levels of paragraphs.

  • Figure Captions
  • Table Headings
  • Page Numbering
  • Lists
  • And More…
