Using Snippet Conditions

In Contributor, you can apply snippet conditions at the snippet level. This lets you separate certain snippet content so it displays in certain snippets in a topic, but not in others. Snippet conditions applied at the snippet level override snippet conditions applied at the topic level in Flare.
