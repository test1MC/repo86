Welcome to MadCap —designed for Flare topic reviews, contributions, and light content development. allows for the following workflows:

  • Review Workflow (Registered Users) Review and edit documents sent by Flare authors.
  • Free Review Workflow Review documents sent by Flare authors (no editing, annotations only).
  • Contribution Workflow Create original content to be contributed to Flare projects.
  • New Document Workflow Create content that can be printed or saved in various formats.
